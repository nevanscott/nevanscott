Bent Quill Players

I just finished playing Sir Jealous Traffick in the Bent Quill Players production of Susanna Centlivre’s The Busybody. Thanks to all who made it out to the show, we had a great time!

Richard Grayson has kindly written some background and an excellent review of the show. Apparently, yours truly “played the blustery Sir Jealous Traffic with aplomb.”

Update: I’m excited to be joining the Bent Quill Players team and looking forward to helping put together more great shows in the future. Stay tuned.
