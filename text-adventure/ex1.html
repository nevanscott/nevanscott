Exemplary Mystery

It is difficult to see but you hear a faint humming on your left. Ahead of you, a bit of light is peaking as if at the end of a long hallway. Just enough light is making its way so that you can see what looks like a notebook about 4 feet in front of you.

What would you like to do?

  • Go back
  • Keep going ahead
  • Examine the notebook
  • Go left
